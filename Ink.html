Diani Beach

Diani Beach is situated close to 30 miles south of Mombasa and it feels like a true tropical island vacation instead of just a portion of the mainland. It is vast, pristine and surrounded by coconut palms. Coral reefs shield it from the massive surf and this makes for the ideal environment to snorkel, swim or float with the lukewarm waters.
